PROJECT USP'S

3.5 ACRES oF MANMADE LAKE

At the heart of the estate lies a breathtaking 3.5-acre man-made lake — designed to create tranquillity, scenic beauty, ideal for kayaking, and a natural microclimate.

VINEYARD EXPERIENCE

Encircling the lake is a beautifully planned vineyard, offering a European countryside charm within your private estate. Evening strolls by the water, vineyard views, and curated open spaces redefine the meaning of peaceful luxury.
